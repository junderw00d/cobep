As the coronavirus pandemic countinues to disrupt our lives, it's a great time to relax and take out our long-neglected reading lists. And while our libraries offer digital ebook collections, nothing beats the classic ink-on-the-page book. How, then, can we safely borrow and lend physical books? That's where the Central Ohio Book Exchange comes in. Here's how it works:

  1. 1 Anyone can check out a book from the catalogue.
  2. 2 The book's owner is alerted and places the book on their porch.
  3. 3 The borrower picks it up.
  4. 4 After two weeks, the borrower returns the book at the owner's house so that others can enjoy it.
  5. 5 Borrowers are encouraged to lend out their own book collection.

coronavirus considerations

Given the current situation, all patrons and lenders should exercise precaution when using this service . We ask that books are wrapped in cardboard and sprayed with disinfectants. Borrowers should wait 12 hours before retrieving books.
